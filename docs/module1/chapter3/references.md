# References for Chapter 3: Simulation-to-Reality in Robotics

## Academic Papers

1. Sadeghi, F., & Levine, S. (2017). CAD2RL: Real single-image flight without a single real image. *Proceedings of the International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA)*, 1241-1248. https://doi.org/10.1109/ICRA.2017.7989138
   - Pioneering work on domain randomization for Sim2Real transfer in robotics

2. Tobin, J., Fong, R., Ray, A., Schneider, J., Zaremba, W., & Abbeel, P. (2017). Domain randomization for transferring deep neural networks from simulation to the real world. *Proceedings of the International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS)*, 23-30. https://doi.org/10.1109/IROS.2017.8202133
   - Foundational paper on domain randomization techniques for Sim2Real transfer

3. James, S., Davison, A. J., & Malis, E. (2019). Transferring deep reinforcement learning with simulated robots to real robots. *IEEE Robotics & Automation Magazine*, 26(1), 42-55. https://doi.org/10.1109/MRA.2018.2882775
   - Comprehensive review of Sim2Real techniques with practical examples

4. Peng, X. B., Andry, A., Zhang, E., Abbeel, P., & Dragan, A. (2018). Sim-to-real transfer of robotic control with dynamics randomization. *Proceedings of the International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA)*, 3803-3810. https://doi.org/10.1109/ICRA.2018.8462895
   - Dynamics randomization approach for robotic control transfer

5. Chebotar, Y., Handa, A., Li, V., Morales, A., Hadsell, R., & Clark, R. (2019). Closing the sim-to-real loop: Adapting simulation randomization with real world experience. *Proceedings of the International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA)*, 8973-8979. https://doi.org/10.1109/ICRA.2019.8793821
   - Adaptive simulation randomization based on real-world feedback
