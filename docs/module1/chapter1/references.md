# References for Chapter 1: Introduction to Physical AI

## Academic Papers

1. Brooks, R. A. (1991). Intelligence without representation. *Artificial Intelligence*, 47(1-3), 139-159. https://doi.org/10.1016/0004-3702(91)90053-M
   - Foundational paper on embodied cognition and the importance of physical interaction in intelligence

2. Pfeifer, R., & Bongard, J. (2006). *How the body shapes the way we think: A new view of intelligence*. MIT Press.
   - Comprehensive exploration of embodied intelligence and how physical form influences cognitive processes

3. Lungarella, M., Metta, G., Pfeifer, R., & Sandini, G. (2003). Developmental robotics: A survey. *Connection Science*, 15(4), 151-190. https://doi.org/10.1080/09540090310001655110
   - Survey on developmental robotics and the role of embodiment in robot learning

4. Siciliano, B., & Khatib, O. (Eds.). (2016). *Springer handbook of robotics* (2nd ed.). Springer.
   - Comprehensive reference on robotics including physical AI and humanoid systems

5. Cheng, G., & Ijspeert, A. J. (2021). Bioinspired legged robotics: From robot learning and biological principles to versatile and robust locomotion. *IEEE Robotics & Automation Magazine*, 28(1), 20-32. https://doi.org/10.1109/MRA.2020.3035950
   - Discussion of physical AI in the context of bioinspired humanoid robotics

## Technical Documentation

6. Open Robotics. (2023). ROS 2 Documentation. *ROS 2*. https://docs.ros.org/en/humble/
   - Official ROS 2 documentation for middleware used in physical AI systems

7. NVIDIA. (2023). Isaac ROS: Accelerated Robot Perception and Navigation. *NVIDIA Developer*. https://developer.nvidia.com/isaac-ros-gems
   - Technical documentation for NVIDIA's robotics platform with Physical AI applications

## Online Resources

8. Pfeifer, R., & Scheier, C. (n.d.). *Understanding Intelligence*. MIT Press. Retrieved from https://mitpress.mit.edu/books/understanding-intelligence
   - Online resource on embodied approaches to artificial intelligence

9. Human Brain Project. (2023). Neurorobotics Platform. *Human Brain Project*. https://www.humanbrainproject.eu/en/explore-the-brain/
   - Platform demonstrating Physical AI through brain-inspired robotics
