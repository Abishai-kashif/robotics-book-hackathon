# References for Chapter 4: Humanoid Robotics Fundamentals

## Academic Papers

1. Kajita, S., & Hirukawa, H. (1997). Humanoid robot motion generation with footprints. *Proceedings of the IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation*, 1088-1093. https://doi.org/10.1109/ROBOT.1997.619432
   - Fundamental work on humanoid robot locomotion and balance control

2. Sardain, P., & Bessonnet, G. (2004). Forces acting on a biped robot. Center of pressure-zero moment point. *IEEE Transactions on Systems, Man, and Cybernetics, Part A: Systems and Humans*, 34(6), 630-637. https://doi.org/10.1109/TSMCA.2004.832832
   - Key paper on balance control and the zero moment point concept for humanoid robots

3. Cheng, G., & Ijspeert, A. J. (2021). Bioinspired legged robotics: From robot learning and biological principles to versatile and robust locomotion. *IEEE Robotics & Automation Magazine*, 28(1), 20-32. https://doi.org/10.1109/MRA.2020.3035950
   - Discussion of bioinspired approaches in humanoid robotics

4. Khatib, O., Park, H., Forrai, A., Docquier, G., Bessonnet, G., & Zeghar, M. (2003). Humanoid robots: Making a humanoid face tracking a ball in a juggling task. *IEEE Robotics & Automation Magazine*, 10(4), 36-44. https://doi.org/10.1109/MRA.2003.1256297
   - Practical example of humanoid robot control and interaction

5. Asano, Y., & Kitano, H. (2003). Humanoid robots as research platforms for socially interactive spaces. *IEEE Intelligent Systems*, 18(1), 18-22. https://doi.org/10.1109/MIS.2003.1167340
   - Discussion of humanoid robots in human-centered environments
