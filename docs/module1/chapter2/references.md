## Academic Papers

1. Quigley, M., Gerkey, B., & Smart, W. D. (2019). ROS by example: A companion book to the ROS tutorials. *CreateSpace Independent Publishing Platform*.

2. Macenski, S., & Manouchehri, A. (2022). Professional Robotics: Developing Real-World ROS 2 Applications. *Apress*.
